How to Upload Your STL and Get a Link

If you want me to print a model you already have, you'll need to share it via a link. Here are free options:

1. Catbox.moe

  1. Go to https://catbox.moe/
  2. Drag your STL file into the upload area.
  3. After it uploads, copy the URL that Catbox gives you.
  4. Paste that URL into the “Paste link to your STL” field on the order form.

2. Google Drive

  1. Upload your STL to Google Drive.
  2. Right-click the file → “Get link”.
  3. Set the link so anyone with the link can view/download.
  4. Paste the link into the order form.

3. Dropbox

  1. Upload your STL to Dropbox.
  2. Click “Share” → “Copy link”.
  3. Paste the link into the order form.

Once I receive your link, I can download your STL and start printing. If you’re commissioning a custom model, you can leave this blank.
